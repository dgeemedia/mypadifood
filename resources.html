API Frequently Asked Questions

What authentication methods does the API support?
Our API supports OAuth 2.0 and API key authentication. For most use cases, we recommend using API keys which you can generate from your dashboard.
What are the API rate limits?
Free plans: 100 requests per hour. Business plans: 1,000 requests per hour. Enterprise plans: Custom rate limits based on your needs.
Can I use the API for mobile apps?
Yes, our REST API is fully compatible with mobile applications. We provide SDKs for iOS and Android to make integration easier.

Common Issues & Solutions

How do I reset my admin password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
Why are my WhatsApp orders not coming through?
First, verify your WhatsApp number is correctly configured in settings. Ensure WhatsApp is installed on your phone and you're logged in. Check if customers are using the correct WhatsApp link from your store.
How do I update my store's currency?
Go to Settings → Business Information → Currency. Select your preferred currency. Note: Changing currency will not convert existing product prices automatically.
Can I change my store's subdomain?
Subdomain changes require support intervention. Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your current subdomain and desired new subdomain. There may be a small administrative fee for this service.

Community Guidelines

What can I share in the community?
Share success stories, ask questions, provide helpful tips, discuss challenges, and offer constructive feedback. Please avoid spam, self-promotion, and sharing sensitive business data.
How often are community events held?
We host monthly webinars, quarterly meetups (virtual and in-person), and weekly Q&A sessions. Check our community calendar for upcoming events.
Can I get help from other users?
Absolutely! Our community is built on mutual support. Many experienced users are happy to help newcomers. For urgent technical issues, we still recommend contacting official support.
